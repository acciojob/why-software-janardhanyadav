Why I want to become a developer?

The decision to become a developer can stem from various motivations, and it's a personal choice that can be influenced by a combination of factors. Here are some common reasons why I choose to pursue a career as a developer:
Passion for Technology: Many people are naturally drawn to technology and have a genuine interest in understanding how software and applications work. If you find joy in solving problems and creating solutions through code, a career in development might be a good fit.
Job Opportunities: The demand for skilled developers is high, and the tech industry offers a wide range of job opportunities. This can lead to job security and the ability to work in various sectors, including finance, healthcare, entertainment, and more.
